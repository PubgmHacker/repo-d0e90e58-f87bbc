## Из отзывов (Rave 1-2★ жалобы, 5★ хвалят)
- Sync drift / desync (особенно long sessions)
- No custom emoji / reactions limited
- Bad UI on some platforms
- No AI companion
- Can't easily watch paid services without login hassle
- Battery drain, ads in free

Teleparty: good for Netflix, but limited platforms, no voice, no AI.

## 5 Killer Features (Plink advantages, not in competitors)
1. **AI Companion with Canvas orb** - reactive Siri-like AI that suggests actions, chats during watch.
2. **Live Plink+ themes** - real video backgrounds (aurora etc) via Metal, not static.
3. **Custom PNG emoji packs** - Plink+ exclusive neon/meme emojis, Telegram long-tap.
4. **Visible sync drift + clock sync v2 protocol** - show ms drift, precise correction.
5. **Host login pattern for cinema + safe fallback** - watch Netflix etc by host auth, with App Store safe mode.

Рекомендация: Emphasize AI + themes + custom emoji in marketing as unique social layer.
